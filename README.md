# medi_ai
MediAI is a user-friendly mobile application designed to help individuals manage their daily health routines efficiently. It acts as a digital assistant for tracking medications, scheduling tasks, setting reminders, and monitoring personal health performance.

Key Features:

Reminders: Get timely notifications for taking medicines or performing health-related tasks.

Task Management: Create and organize daily health tasks with priority levels and due times.

Performance Tracking: Monitor your health habits over time and gain insights into your progress.

Intuitive Interface: A clean and responsive design that’s easy to navigate for users of all ages.

Notifications & Alerts: Stay on top of your health routine with instant alerts.

MediAI aims to simplify personal health management, reduce missed medications, and encourage consistent, healthy habits. Whether you’re managing your own schedule or helping someone else, MediAI ensures that important tasks and health routines are never overlooked.
